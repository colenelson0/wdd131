Our story

CDrabbit was established in 2024 by web developer and part-time music producer, Cole Nelson. As the general population continues to depend more on streaming services and less on physical media, the need for preservation grows as well. Our goal is to make music CDs easier to access so that music lovers can cherish their favorite songs fear of them disappearing from streaming services.
